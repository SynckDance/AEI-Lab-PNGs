Ekombi enters the archive as situated practice, passes through QTC and SAM encoding, is grounded within Atomic Confluences, compared to related Nigerian and diaspora dances, reviewed by the Custodian, and stored as a living node. Outputs include StoryMaps, motion replays, comparative dashboards, and pedagogical tools—all open to community correction.
